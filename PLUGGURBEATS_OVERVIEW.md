# PluggurBeats — Platform Overview

## What Is PluggurBeats?

PluggurBeats is a music pitching platform built for independent producers. Instead of cold-emailing A&Rs and hoping for the best, you upload your beats, submit campaigns, and our team routes your music directly to the right people — verified A&R reps, artist managers, and music supervisors.

Everything is organized through your producer dashboard, available on the web and as a native iPhone app.

---

## Who Is It For?

- **Independent producers** who want their beats heard by real industry contacts
- **Beat makers** across all genres: Trap, Drill, R&B, Pop, Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, and more
- Anyone tired of social media noise and cold DMs — PluggurBeats puts your music in the right inboxes

---

## How It Works

### 1. Create an Account
Sign up free. You get a profile, a dashboard, and immediate access to Loop Drops.

### 2. Subscribe to a Plan
To run pitch campaigns, you need a Plugg or Pro subscription. Plans give you pitch credits every month that you spend to submit beats.

### 3. Build a Campaign
- Name your beats, pick your genre and BPM
- Upload your audio files (MP3 or WAV — untagged mixes pitch best)
- Add any collaborators (co-producers, writers, vocalists)
- Submit for staff review

### 4. Staff Review
Every campaign is reviewed by the PluggurBeats team before anything goes out. No beats reach industry contacts until a staff member approves the campaign. This keeps quality high and protects your music.

### 5. Your Beats Get Pitched
Once approved, your campaign is routed based on your plan:
- **Plugg subscribers** — beats are added to the **Verified Library**, a curated catalog that verified A&Rs and artists actively browse
- **Pro subscribers** — beats are both added to the Verified Library AND emailed directly to A&R reps, artist managers, and music supervisors in your selected desk lanes

### 6. Track Everything
Your analytics dashboard shows you exactly what's happening — which campaigns are active, which beats got viewed or downloaded, and (on Pro) who opened your email blast.

## The Verified Library

The Verified Library is a curated catalog of approved producer beats that verified industry contacts can browse at any time. Think of it as your portfolio inside the platform — but only people who matter can see it.

- Every approved Plugg or Pro campaign lands here automatically
- A&Rs, artists, and managers have verified accounts and browse by genre, BPM, and tags
- You can see who viewed and downloaded each beat in your analytics
- It's always live — your catalog grows with every approved campaign

---

## Pro Desk Lanes

Pro subscribers can select up to **5 desk lanes** per campaign. Lanes route your approved beats to specific types of industry contacts via email blast:

- **Trap A-list** — top-tier rap artist desks
- **Trap Rising** — rising rap acts
- **R&B A-list** — established R&B artists
- **R&B Rising** — emerging R&B
- **Pop (Major)** — major label pop desks
- **Afrobeats Rising** — Afrobeats circuit
- **Drill Rising** — UK and US drill scenes
- **Reggaeton Rising** — Latin/reggaeton market
- **Major Label A&R** — Trap and Pop A&R reps at majors
- **Indie / Distribution A&R** — indie labels and distributors
- **Sync / Music Supervisors** — film, TV, and ad placements
- **Artist Management** — manager contacts across all genres

Desk selection costs no extra credits — you only spend 1 credit per beat regardless of how many lanes you add.

## Loop Drops

Loop Drops is a separate marketplace on the platform where producers can submit short loop samples to a shared pool. Verified artists and producers can browse and use them.

- Anyone can submit loops (even Free users, using loop credits)
- Verified members can pull loops from the pool
- You can see who played or downloaded your loops in your analytics

---

## Analytics

Your analytics dashboard is organized by campaign so you can track performance at every level:

**Campaign level** — date submitted, status, total opens and downloads across all beats

**Beat level** — expand any campaign to see each individual beat and its activity:
- **Verified library** — how many verified users viewed or downloaded this beat from the library, and who they are
- **Email engagement** (Pro only) — which contacts opened the email, who downloaded the ZIP, with timestamps

**Loop activity** — separate section showing who played or downloaded your loop submissions

All viewer identities are protected — you'll see usernames of platform members, or a first name for non-members. Raw emails and phone numbers are never shown.

---

## Staff Review Process

Every campaign goes through human review before pitching. Here's what happens:

1. You submit a campaign — status shows **"Under review"**
2. Staff listens to your beats, checks audio quality and metadata
3. If **approved** — beats are pitched (added to Verified Library and/or emailed to contacts depending on your plan)
4. If **rejected** — you get a reason and your credits are refunded automatically

This review step exists to protect the quality of the library and maintain relationships with industry contacts. Campaigns with properly tagged, untagged-mix audio files get approved fastest.

## Paperwork (Split Sheets)

The dashboard includes a built-in split sheet generator. If you have collaborators on a beat, you can create a legally formatted split sheet, assign ownership percentages, and send it to all parties for e-signature via DocuSign — without leaving the platform.

---

## Privacy & Data

- Producer emails and phone numbers are **never shared** with industry contacts
- Industry contact emails are **never shown** to producers
- Analytics show engagement (opens, downloads, views) and identity (username or first name) — not raw contact info
- Your beats are stored securely and only accessible via time-limited signed links
