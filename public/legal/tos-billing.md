# Subscription, Credits & Billing Terms

Last updated: June 30, 2026

These terms apply when you subscribe, buy credits, use credits, join a paid tier, or buy add-ons on PluggurBeats. These terms supplement the Base Terms of Service.

## 1. Paid Plans

PluggurBeats offers paid plans — Plugg and Pro — along with free access. Paid plans provide different access to submissions, request targeting, verified library features, analytics, credit allowances, and review workflows. Plan names, prices, credit allowances, and features may change. The checkout screen controls the current price and features at the time of purchase.

## 2. Paid Plans and Verified Roles Are Separate

Paid subscription status is separate from verified profile role. A paid plan controls commercial access and plan-gated features. A verified profile role describes marketplace identity, such as Producer, Producer+, Artist+, A&R, or A&R++. Buying a paid plan does not guarantee verification, a specific role, A&R access, placements, responses, downloads, or industry outcomes.

## 3. Credits

PluggurBeats uses credits — pitch credits and loop credits — which may be included in subscriptions, purchased in packs, or granted by staff. Credits have no cash value, are not legal tender, and cannot be transferred, resold, redeemed for cash, or used outside PluggurBeats.

Credits are used for: submitting beat campaigns, submitting loops, optional library listing for targeted submissions, and rush queue add-ons.

## 4. Credit Deductions

Credits are deducted server-side in atomic transactions. You must have enough credits for the requested action. If a transaction fails, the action will not complete. If credits are deducted incorrectly due to a platform error, PluggurBeats may adjust your balance.

## 5. Monthly Allowances

Subscriptions include monthly credits. Credits roll over month to month up to a cap of 3x the monthly grant amount. The cap prevents unlimited accumulation. Upon cancellation, unused credits may no longer be usable after the billing period ends.

Current monthly grants:
- Free: 0 pitch credits, 5 loop credits
- Plugg: 15 pitch credits, 20 loop credits
- Pro: 50 pitch credits, 60 loop credits

## 6. Credit Packs

Credit packs are one-time purchases. Credit packs are non-refundable except where required by applicable law and do not expire.

## 7. Rush Queue and Add-Ons

Rush queue and other add-ons may cost additional credits or fees. Rush queue affects review priority but does not guarantee approval, placement, response, download, or any outcome.

## 8. Billing Provider

Payments are processed by Stripe. By purchasing, you authorize PluggurBeats and Stripe to charge your selected payment method for applicable fees, taxes, renewals, and add-ons. Stripe's terms may also apply.

## 9. Renewals and Cancellation

Subscriptions renew automatically until canceled. You are responsible for canceling before renewal if you do not want to be charged again. After cancellation, access to paid features may continue until the end of the current billing period.

## 10. Refunds

Unless required by law or expressly stated at checkout, payments for subscriptions, credits, credit packs, rush add-ons, and digital services are non-refundable. PluggurBeats may issue credits or adjustments at its discretion, but doing so once does not create an obligation to do so again.

## 11. Failed Payments

If a payment fails, PluggurBeats may suspend or limit paid features, credit allowances, submission access, downloads, and request targeting.
