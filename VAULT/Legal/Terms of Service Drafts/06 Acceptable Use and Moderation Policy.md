# Acceptable Use and Moderation Policy

Draft for attorney review. This policy applies to all users and all content.

## 1. Prohibited Content and Conduct

You may not use PluggurBeats to:

- upload stolen beats, loops, vocals, samples, or artwork;
- present uncleared samples or third-party content as cleared;
- infringe copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, contract, publishing, master, or other rights;
- impersonate artists, A&Rs, labels, staff, producers, managers, companies, or other people;
- create fake verified identities or misleading role claims;
- harass, threaten, abuse, dox, exploit, or discriminate against others;
- spam requests, submissions, comments, messages, emails, or notifications;
- scrape, harvest, expose, infer, sell, or misuse private contact information;
- use bots, automation, credential stuffing, scraping, fake accounts, or download abuse;
- upload malware, corrupted files, deceptive files, or harmful code;
- manipulate analytics, views, downloads, pulls, credits, subscriptions, referrals, or reviews;
- commit payment fraud, credit fraud, chargeback abuse, or refund abuse;
- bypass access controls, rate limits, verification gates, role gates, or plan gates;
- interfere with the service or another user's use of the service;
- use content downloaded from PluggurBeats beyond the rights granted.

## 2. Music Rights Abuse

PluggurBeats may remove content or suspend accounts based on suspected stolen beats, disputed samples, uncleared collaborations, Content ID abuse, false ownership claims, or repeated rights complaints.

## 3. Contact Abuse

Private A&R, artist, label, user, and internal contact data is protected. Attempts to expose, scrape, sell, share, or route around private contact protection may result in immediate removal.

## 4. Staff Enforcement

PluggurBeats may investigate, warn, restrict, hide, remove, reject, archive, delete, suspend, terminate, ban, adjust credits, disable downloads, disable requests, disable verification, or report conduct to third parties or authorities where appropriate.

## 5. No Obligation to Monitor

PluggurBeats may moderate content but is not obligated to monitor all content or activity. Failure to remove content immediately does not mean PluggurBeats approves it or waives enforcement rights.

## 6. Reporting

Users should be able to report abuse, infringement, fake identity, contact misuse, stolen beats, spam, or harmful behavior through [report email/form/link].
