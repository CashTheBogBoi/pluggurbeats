# DMCA and Copyright Policy

Draft for attorney review. This should be reviewed before publication, especially if PluggurBeats seeks DMCA safe-harbor protection.

## 1. Copyright Respect

PluggurBeats respects copyright and expects users to upload and submit only content they own or have permission to use.

## 2. Takedown Notices

If you believe content on PluggurBeats infringes your copyright, send a written notice to [copyright agent/contact] with:

- your name, company if applicable, mailing address, phone number, and email;
- identification of the copyrighted work you claim was infringed;
- identification of the material you claim is infringing and enough information for us to locate it;
- a statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law;
- a statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act for the owner;
- your physical or electronic signature.

## 3. Response to Notices

PluggurBeats may remove or disable access to allegedly infringing material and may notify the user who uploaded it.

## 4. Counter-Notices

If your content was removed due to a copyright notice and you believe it was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may send a counter-notice to [copyright agent/contact] with:

- your name, mailing address, phone number, and email;
- identification of the removed material and where it appeared before removal;
- a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the material was removed or disabled by mistake or misidentification;
- a statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court for your address, or if outside the United States, the jurisdiction where PluggurBeats is located;
- a statement that you will accept service of process from the person who submitted the original notice or their agent;
- your physical or electronic signature.

## 5. Repeat Infringers

PluggurBeats may terminate or restrict users who repeatedly infringe copyrights or repeatedly receive valid copyright complaints.

## 6. Rights Disputes

PluggurBeats may remove or restrict content involved in ownership, sample, collaborator, publishing, master, or licensing disputes even if the dispute is not a formal DMCA notice.

## 7. Designated Agent

Counsel should decide whether to register a designated DMCA agent with the U.S. Copyright Office and publish the registered agent information here.
