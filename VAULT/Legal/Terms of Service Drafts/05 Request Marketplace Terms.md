# Request Marketplace Terms

Draft for attorney review. These terms apply when users create, view, submit to, moderate, or receive submissions through campaign requests, loop requests, beat requests, announcements, or similar marketplace features.

## 1. Verified Request Creators

PluggurBeats may allow verified users to create requests. Verified producers may be limited to loop requests. Verified artists and A&Rs may be allowed to request beats, loops, or both. Staff may post announcements or platform requests.

PluggurBeats may change who can create requests and what types of requests they can create.

## 2. Request Content

Request creators are responsible for request titles, briefs, genres, tags, references, deadlines, budgets, label names, and other request details. Request content must be accurate, lawful, non-deceptive, and compliant with the Acceptable Use and Moderation Policy.

Request creators may not use requests to collect private contact data, evade platform workflows, spam users, impersonate labels or A&Rs, or solicit illegal or infringing content.

## 3. Role and Plan Gating

Submission access may depend on both paid plan and verified role. Current product direction:

- Free users may submit only to Producer requests.
- Plugg users may submit to Producer, Producer+, Artist, Artist+, and A&R requests.
- Pro users may submit to all verified role tiers.

PluggurBeats may change role gates, plan gates, request visibility, or submission rules.

## 4. A&R Label Names

A&R users may display an associated label name where allowed. Display of a label name does not necessarily prove employment, agency, authority, signing power, or the ability to bind a label unless separately verified by PluggurBeats or the label.

Users may not impersonate A&Rs, labels, artists, managers, producers, or companies.

## 5. Private Contact Protection

Request cards and request flows should not expose private emails, phone numbers, contact lists, or internal desk mappings. Pro-to-A&R delivery, if offered, should use server-side lookup and platform-controlled delivery without exposing the A&R's private contact information to the submitting user.

Users may not attempt to bypass this protection.

## 6. Targeted Submissions

Submissions made to a specific request are delivered to that request or request owner according to platform workflows. Targeted beat submissions are exclusive to the requester by default and do not enter the public Verified library unless the uploader chooses optional library listing and pays the required credits.

## 7. Request Analytics

PluggurBeats may track request views, submissions, approvals, email deliveries, opens, clicks, conversion, and other analytics. Analytics may be approximate and may change over time. PluggurBeats does not guarantee analytics accuracy, delivery, opens, responses, or outcomes.

## 8. Moderation

PluggurBeats may pin, unpin, close, delete, hide, reject, archive, or moderate requests. Staff may create announcements and manage request visibility. Request creators may receive owner controls such as edit, close, or archive if PluggurBeats offers them.

## 9. No Guaranteed Outcome

Posting a request does not guarantee quality submissions. Submitting to a request does not guarantee review, response, placement, license, release, income, or credit.
