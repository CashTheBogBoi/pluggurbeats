# PluggurBeats Legal Terms Map

Draft for attorney review. This packet is designed as modular terms for different user actions, without adding anything to the website yet.

## Recommended Document Stack

1. [[01 Base Terms of Service]]
   - Applies to all account creation and general use.
   - Covers accounts, eligibility, platform role, moderation, disclaimers, liability, termination, and governing terms.

2. [[02 Subscription Credits and Billing Terms]]
   - Applies when a user starts a paid plan, buys credits, uses rush queue, or receives monthly allowances.
   - Covers Plugg/Pro subscriptions, pitch credits, loop credits, credit packs, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and expiration.

3. [[03 Upload and Beat Submission Agreement]]
   - Applies when a producer uploads beats, loops, campaigns, or request-targeted submissions.
   - Covers ownership promises, sample/collaborator clearance, platform license, staff review, rejection, removal, targeted exclusivity, and optional library listing.

4. [[04 Verified User Download and Review Terms]]
   - Applies when verified artists, A&Rs, labels, or other verified users stream, download, export, pull, or review beats/loops.
   - Clarifies that downloads are for evaluation/review only unless a separate license exists.

5. [[05 Request Marketplace Terms]]
   - Applies when verified users post requests or when users submit to those requests.
   - Covers verified roles, A&R label names, request content, role/plan gating, no contact exposure, analytics, and Pro-to-A&R delivery.

6. [[06 Acceptable Use and Moderation Policy]]
   - Applies to everyone.
   - Covers prohibited content and behavior, fake identities, spam, scraping, bot downloads, harassment, fraud, and staff enforcement.

7. [[07 DMCA and Copyright Policy]]
   - Applies to copyright complaints and repeat infringer handling.
   - Should be reviewed by counsel before launch and paired with a designated DMCA agent if safe-harbor protection is desired.

8. [[08 Email and Notifications Policy]]
   - Applies to transactional emails, campaign/request delivery, push notifications, unsubscribe/suppression handling, and notification preferences.

## Product Facts These Terms Must Match

- PluggurBeats is a curated beat and loop pitching platform, not a guaranteed placement service.
- Staff reviews campaigns and loops before they enter public verified surfaces.
- Targeted request submissions are exclusive to the requester by default.
- A targeted submission enters the public Verified library only if the producer chooses the extra library-listing option and pays the extra credit cost.
- Downloads/exports do not automatically grant commercial release rights.
- Paid tiers and verified profile roles are separate.
- A&R and artist contact information must remain private and server-side.
- Staff can moderate, remove, reject, approve, ban, and adjust access to protect the service.

## Open Business Decisions For Counsel

- Final legal entity name and address.
- Minimum age and whether minors are allowed.
- Governing law and venue.
- Whether to include arbitration and class action waiver.
- Subscription refund policy.
- Credit expiration and rollover policy.
- What happens to unused credits after cancellation or account termination.
- Whether rush fees are refundable if rejected or delayed.
- Whether approved library downloads are always evaluation-only or sometimes include preset license terms.
- DMCA agent registration and public copyright contact address.
- Business email address and physical mailing address for email compliance.
