# Subscription Credits and Billing Terms

Draft for attorney review. These terms apply when a user subscribes, buys credits, uses credits, joins a paid tier, or buys add-ons.

## 1. Paid Plans

PluggurBeats may offer paid plans such as Plugg and Pro, along with free access. Paid plans may provide different access to submissions, request targeting, verified library features, analytics, credit allowances, review workflows, or other features.

Paid plan names, prices, credit allowances, submission access, and features may change over time. The checkout screen or plan page should control the current price and included features at the time of purchase.

## 2. Paid Plans and Verified Roles Are Separate

Paid subscription status is separate from verified profile role. A paid plan controls commercial access and plan-gated features. A verified profile role describes marketplace identity or status, such as Producer, Producer+, Producer++, Artist, Artist+, Artist++, A&R, A&R+, or A&R++.

Buying a paid plan does not guarantee verification, a specific role, A&R access, placements, responses, downloads, or industry outcomes.

## 3. Credits

PluggurBeats may use credits, including pitch credits and loop credits. Credits may be included in subscriptions, purchased in packs, granted manually, refunded by staff workflows, or used for actions such as submitting campaigns, submitting loops, rush queue, or optional library listing.

Credits have no cash value, are not legal tender, and cannot be transferred, resold, redeemed for cash, or used outside PluggurBeats unless we expressly allow it.

## 4. Credit Deductions

Credits are deducted server-side. A user must have enough credits for the requested action. If a transaction fails, the action may not complete. If credits are deducted incorrectly due to platform error, PluggurBeats may adjust the credit balance.

Examples of credit uses may include:

- submitting beats to a request;
- submitting loops;
- rush queue add-ons;
- optional library listing for targeted submissions;
- credit-gated delivery or campaign features.
