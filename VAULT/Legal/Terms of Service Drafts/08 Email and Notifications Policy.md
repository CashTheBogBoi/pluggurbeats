# Email and Notifications Policy

Draft for attorney review. This policy applies to emails, push notifications, campaign delivery, request delivery, announcements, and notification preferences.

## 1. Types of Messages

PluggurBeats may send:

- account, login, security, billing, subscription, and support messages;
- submission status messages, approval/rejection messages, and staff review updates;
- request marketplace messages, including direct submissions and open request notices;
- analytics messages such as views, downloads, pulls, and delivery events;
- push notifications if enabled;
- optional marketing or promotional messages where permitted.

## 2. Transactional Messages

Some messages are transactional or service-related and may be necessary to provide the service. These may include billing notices, password/security messages, submission status, request delivery, moderation notices, and account updates.

## 3. Optional Messages

Some messages may be optional, such as open request alerts, announcements, marketing, promotional messages, or non-essential push notifications. Users should be able to manage preferences where required or where PluggurBeats offers controls.

## 4. Pro-to-A&R Delivery

If PluggurBeats sends Pro submissions to A&Rs, artists, or labels by email or similar delivery, private recipient contact information should remain server-side and should not be exposed to the submitting user.

PluggurBeats should maintain suppression and opt-out controls for recipients where required.

## 5. Email Compliance

Commercial or promotional emails should use truthful sender information, non-deceptive subject lines, accurate content, a valid physical mailing address where required, and a clear opt-out method where required.

PluggurBeats should honor opt-out requests within the legally required time and maintain suppression lists.

## 6. Push Notifications

Push notifications may include beat viewed, beat downloaded, direct submission, open request, and announcement events. Users may need to enable notifications on their device and may disable notifications through device settings or available in-app controls.

## 7. Delivery Not Guaranteed

PluggurBeats does not guarantee that emails, push notifications, request delivery, alerts, or analytics events will be delivered, opened, clicked, read, or acted on.

## 8. Logs and Analytics

PluggurBeats may log message delivery, opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, notification tokens, device records, and related analytics to operate and improve the service and maintain compliance.
