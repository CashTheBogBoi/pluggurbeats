# Verified User Download and Review Terms

Draft for attorney review. These terms apply when a verified user, A&R, artist, label, creator, producer, or other authorized user streams, previews, downloads, exports, pulls, reviews, or receives beats, loops, campaigns, or request submissions.

## 1. Evaluation and Review Access

Unless PluggurBeats expressly states otherwise in a separate license or transaction, access to beats, loops, downloads, exports, and previews is for evaluation, review, internal consideration, creative exploration, request response, and platform workflow purposes only.

Downloading or exporting a file does not automatically grant commercial release rights.

## 2. No Automatic License

Unless a separate written license exists, you may not:

- commercially release music using downloaded beats or loops;
- resell, redistribute, sublicense, lease, sell, upload, or transfer downloaded files;
- claim ownership of downloaded files;
- register downloaded files with Content ID or similar systems against the uploader;
- distribute downloaded files to unauthorized third parties;
- remove producer credits or metadata in a misleading way;
- use downloaded files in a way that violates the uploader's rights or PluggurBeats policies.

## 3. Separate Licenses

If you want to commercially release, distribute, monetize, or otherwise exploit a beat or loop, you are responsible for obtaining a separate license from the rights holder unless PluggurBeats provides an explicit licensing flow that says otherwise.

PluggurBeats is not responsible for negotiating, verifying, enforcing, or clearing separate licenses unless expressly agreed in writing.

## 4. Request Inbox Access

If you post a request and receive targeted submissions, those submissions may be available in your inbound inbox for review. Targeted submissions are exclusive to you by default but still remain owned by their uploader or applicable rights holders unless a separate license is completed.

## 5. Loop Pulls

Exclusive loops may become unavailable after being pulled. Shared loops may remain available to multiple pullers. Pulling a loop does not automatically grant commercial release rights unless a separate license or explicit platform license says so.

## 6. Confidentiality and Private Access

You may receive access to submissions, previews, private request responses, or other content that is not publicly listed. You may not leak, scrape, repost, sell, redistribute, or misuse private submissions or files.

## 7. Abuse

Misuse of downloads, exports, pulls, private submissions, or Content ID systems may result in loss of verified access, removal from the service, account termination, and potential legal claims.
