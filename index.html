Straight answers

Before you buy

Do you guarantee a placement?

No, and you should be careful with anyone who does. We guarantee a defined number of qualified, hand-picked pitches to real decision-makers in your genre, plus status updates on each one. Whether a record gets cut depends on the song, the timing and the artist.

How do the artists actually hear my beat?

Through direct pitches to the people who build the records — artists' production teams, managers and label A&Rs we already work with. We pitch on a best-effort basis and never imply endorsement by anyone we contact.

What do I need to send?

An untagged, mixed beat (WAV preferred), the BPM and key, and the lane you're aiming at. Cleaner files pitch better.

Who keeps the publishing?

You do. We're a pitching service, not a publisher — we don't take points on your beat. If a placement happens, you negotiate the split directly with the artist's team.

How fast do you start?

First pitches typically go out within 72 hours of your files clearing review.

What does "guaranteed pitches" mean?

Each package guarantees a set number of qualified pitches to real, matched desks, with per-pitch status tracking. If we don't deliver the count, we top up or refund the difference. We guarantee delivery and reporting — never a placement.
